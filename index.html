About

Welcome to my portfolio website this is my creation of an interactive online resource. Throughout the site you will find three original photographs, a 2 minute audio piece, a 2 minute video

and a design log featuring an example infographic. I am currently studying Journalism Studies at the University Academy 92 (UA92) this website is part of my second module: Media Production.

Photography

For the photography section of our assignment we were tasked with taking pictures in and around Manchester. These pictures are supposed to highlight the photography skills and practices I have learnt throughout the course. We decided on a theme of street art for our photography and went to the Northern Quarter of Manchester to take photographs.

Video

For the video section of our assignment we were tasked with filming and editing a 2 minute TV video. We were tasked with finding someone interesting to interview, we decided to interview Mark Richardson. We discovered his love of skiing and an interesting tale of celebrities on the slopes.

Audio

For the audio section of our assignment we were tasked with recording and editing a 2 minute audio interview, we interviewed Corey Jones about health and fitness.
