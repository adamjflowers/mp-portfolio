Design Log Week Two


Posted by Adam Flowers on November 22nd, 2019

 

Monday 18th November

What did I learn and why?

In web design I  made changes to the colours on my webpage; using the CSS stylesheet. In photography we used the Canon website to practice different photography techniques like changing the F stop and contrast.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will ensure my final website looks the part by making changes to the CSS. In photography I can further my skills by changing various settings to improve my final portfolio images.

Tuesday 19th November

What did I learn and why?

I swapped the images on my website for free to use images from the open source website Unsplash. These will act as placeholders for my own work before I upload it. During photography we learnt the difference between RAW and JPEG images and then began to learn the basics of Adobe Photoshop.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will be able to use these skills to upload my own images to my final website. Photo editing will allow me to be able to make sure my final photos are of the best possible quality.

Wednesday 20th November

What did I learn and why?

For Digital Wednesday I read through guides on HTML, CSS and dealing with files. I then wrote an entry for a collaborative glossary defining a term, I had learnt from reading the guides. This research helped me to fill in the gaps in my knowledge and plan changes to my site.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will be able to code better when it comes to my final website.

Thursday 21st November

What did I learn and why?

During web design I ensured all my pages were matching and linking correctly and that they all featured placeholders. In Photography we went into Manchester city centre and took some photographs to practice our photography skills.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will make sure my final website is linked probably and will have the skills to adapt images for site. I will use the experience I have gained to take further better photos for the site.

Friday 22nd November

What did I learn and why?

I filled in my Digital Log and submitted it alongside my prototype website.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will make sure I complete all deadlines.
