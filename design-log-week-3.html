Design Log Week Three


Posted by Adam Flowers on November 29th, 2019

 

Monday 25th November

What did I learn and why?

My prototype website was transformed into by portfolio website by the process of duplication. This will allow me to track the sites progress in development. We also began to explore Colour theory to help us to choose colours for our own site. In the afternoon we began using audio equipment.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

I will use colour theory to change the colour scheme of my website to improve accessibility and aesthetics. Being familiar with the audio equipment will make our final recording much easier.

Tuesday 26th November

What did I learn and why?

In web design we were taught about alternative text to increase the accessibility of our website. In our audio production lesson we learnt about interviewing and the correct way to ask questions. .

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

Applying what I learned in web design will be a simple process of editing the alt tags in atom for the images on my web site.

Wednesday 27th November

What did I learn and why?

For digital Wednesday I had to research websites with good and bad accessibility. I practiced photographic techniques in my own time.  

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

Using other websites as a comparison and potential inspiration for my own will allow me to adapt and change my website in a suitable manner. Practicing my photography skills will help me to take quality photos for my final website.

Thursday 28th November

What did I learn and why?       

Exporting for web will be key for me as it will allow my images to be of the best possible quality without decreasing the overall quality.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

Exporting for web will be key for me as it will allow my images to be of the best possible quality without decreasing the overall quality. Planning for our interview will mean we are more prepared and more likely to produce a quality piece of audio.

Friday 29th November

What did I learn and why?

In web design our tutor gave us various code snippets that we could incorporate onto our website, I used different versions on most of my pages. In audio we did some practice recording.

How will I implement this learning into my final portfolio?

The use of the code snippets will make my website accessible with each page being more appropriate for content it is displaying. Practicing using the audio equipment will allow me to produce a higher quality end product.
